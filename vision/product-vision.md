# Avi Product Vision

## Current State
Avi is connected to all social channels (YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, X, Medium) and provides analytics tracking with an AI-powered content assistant.

## Future Agents & Tools

### 1. Content Ideas Agent
An AI agent that taps into connected social channels and analyzes performance data to proactively suggest content ideas to post. The agent will:
- Analyze trending topics across platforms
- Review what's performing well on your channels
- Suggest timely content ideas based on audience engagement patterns
- Provide platform-specific recommendations

### 2. Booking Tool
A scheduling tool connected to Google Calendar for managing content creator workflows:
- Schedule content creation sessions
- Manage collaboration meetings
- Track posting schedules across platforms
- Coordinate with team members or clients

### 3. Content Review Tool
An AI agent that can watch videos and suggest edits:
- Analyze video content for pacing and engagement
- Suggest cuts and improvements
- Identify sections that may lose viewer attention
- Recommend hooks, transitions, and calls-to-action
- Provide feedback on audio/visual quality

### 4. Video Editing Agent (Ultimate Goal)
An autonomous agent that can actually edit videos:
- Apply suggested edits automatically
- Add captions and subtitles
- Insert transitions and effects
- Optimize for different platforms (vertical for TikTok/Reels, horizontal for YouTube)
- Generate thumbnails and preview clips
